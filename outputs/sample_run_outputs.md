### Question

How many annual leave days can an employee carry forward?

### Answer

An employee can carry forward a maximum of **6 unused annual leave days** into the next calendar year. Carry-forward leave must be used by **31 March** of the next calendar year. Any carried-forward leave not used by 31 March will lapse unless an exception is approved by HR.

### Question

Can I use my personal laptop for office work?

### Answer

According to the IT Security Policy (Source File: it_security_policy.md, Chunk ID: it_security_policy.md_chunk_7), employees should not use personal laptops for regular office work. However, personal laptop use is allowed under certain conditions, such as temporary business need, manager approval, IT security approval, no confidential or customer data stored locally, and the device meeting minimum security requirements. 

To obtain approval for using a personal laptop for office work, you would need to follow the approval process outlined in the policy, which includes obtaining manager approval and IT security approval. 

Please note that the use of personal laptops for customer data, production access, or confidential project documents requires explicit IT security approval. 

It is recommended that you review the IT Security Policy in its entirety and consult with your manager and IT security team to ensure compliance with the policy.

### Question

What approvals are needed for international travel?

### Answer

For international travel, the following approvals are needed:
- Reporting manager approval
- Business unit head approval
- Finance approval
- Travel desk confirmation before ticket booking

International travel requests must be submitted at least 15 working days before the travel date, except in urgent client situations.

### Question

Can customer data be uploaded to a public AI tool?

### Answer

No, customer data cannot be uploaded to a public AI tool. According to the AI Usage Policy (Source File: ai_usage_policy.md, Chunk ID: ai_usage_policy.md_chunk_0), public AI tools may be used only for low-risk tasks that do not involve confidential, personal, customer, financial, legal, security, or regulated data. Additionally, the policy states that employees must not upload customer data, personal data, confidential company data, source code, production logs, contracts, financial data, credentials, or security findings to public AI tools (Source File: ai_usage_policy.md, Chunk ID: ai_usage_policy.md_chunk_0).
