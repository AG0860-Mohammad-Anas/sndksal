### Question

Can I use my personal laptop for office work?

### Answer

You should not use your personal laptop for regular office work, but you can use it in exceptional cases with manager and IT security approval, and if the device meets minimum security requirements. This is according to the IT Security Policy (IT-SEC-001, Version 1.0) as stated in the it_security_policy.md file, chunk it_security_policy.md_chunk_7.

## 2026-06-19 16:03:08

### Question

What approvals are needed for international travel?

### Answer

International business travel requires reporting manager approval, business unit head approval, finance approval, and travel desk confirmation before ticket booking. International travel requests must be submitted at least 15 working days before the travel date except in urgent client situations.

### Question

Will my reimbursement definitely be approved?

### Answer

No, reimbursement approval is not guaranteed. According to the policy, reimbursement claims are eligible only if they meet specific conditions, such as being business-related, having prior approval where required, and being submitted within the defined timeline. Finance may reject claims that do not meet these conditions.
