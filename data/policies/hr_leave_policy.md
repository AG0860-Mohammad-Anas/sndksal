# HR Leave Policy
**Policy ID:** HR-LV-001
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01
**Policy Owner:** Human Resources

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines leave entitlement, leave approval rules, carry-forward limits, cancellation rules, and unpaid leave requirements for full-time employees.

## 2. Annual Leave Entitlement
Full-time employees are eligible for **24 working days of annual leave per calendar year**.

Annual leave is accrued monthly and can be used after completion of the employee's probation period unless otherwise approved by HR.

## 3. Carry-Forward Rules
Employees may carry forward a maximum of **6 unused annual leave days** into the next calendar year.

Carry-forward leave must be used by **31 March** of the next calendar year. Any carried-forward leave not used by 31 March will lapse unless an exception is approved by HR.

## 4. Sick Leave
Employees are eligible for **10 working days of sick leave per calendar year**.

A medical certificate is required if sick leave exceeds **2 consecutive working days**.

## 5. Leave Approval Workflow
Annual leave requests must be submitted in the HR system at least **5 working days before** the planned leave date.

The employee's reporting manager must approve annual leave before the leave is considered confirmed.

Leave requests longer than **10 consecutive working days** require both manager approval and HR approval.

## 6. Unpaid Leave
Unpaid leave may be requested when paid leave balance is insufficient.

Unpaid leave requires approval from the reporting manager and HR. Approval is not automatic and depends on business continuity requirements.

## 7. Leave Cancellation
Approved leave can be cancelled by the employee before the leave start date.

If cancellation happens after travel or reimbursement bookings have been made, the employee must follow the applicable travel or reimbursement policy.

## 8. Policy Exceptions
Exceptions to this policy require written approval from HR.

The policy does not guarantee approval of any leave request. Approval depends on leave balance, business needs, manager approval, and HR review where applicable.
