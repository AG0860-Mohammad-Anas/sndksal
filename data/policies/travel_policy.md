# Travel Policy
**Policy ID:** TRV-001
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01
**Policy Owner:** Administration and Finance

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines rules for domestic travel, international travel, same-day business travel, travel approvals, booking requirements, and post-travel documentation.

## 2. Domestic Business Travel
Domestic business travel must be approved by the employee's reporting manager before travel is booked.

Travel requests should include:
- business purpose
- destination
- travel dates
- estimated cost
- client or project reference, if applicable

## 3. Same-Day Domestic Business Travel
Same-day domestic business travel is allowed when the employee returns to their home city on the same calendar day.

For same-day domestic travel:
- manager approval is required before travel
- meal reimbursement is governed by the Reimbursement Policy
- local transport reimbursement requires receipts
- hotel reimbursement is not applicable unless overnight stay is approved separately

## 4. Overnight Domestic Travel
Overnight domestic travel requires manager approval.

Hotel bookings should use company-approved vendors where available. If a company-approved vendor is not available, the employee must submit a valid hotel invoice for reimbursement.

## 5. International Business Travel
International business travel requires:
- reporting manager approval
- business unit head approval
- finance approval
- travel desk confirmation before ticket booking

International travel requests must be submitted at least **15 working days before** the travel date except in urgent client situations.

## 6. Travel Booking
Employees should not book travel personally unless the travel desk has approved self-booking.

Travel booked without approval may not be reimbursed.

## 7. Travel Changes and Cancellation
Changes to approved travel must be communicated to the manager and travel desk.

Additional charges caused by personal preference changes may not be reimbursed.

## 8. Policy Limitations
This policy defines travel approval requirements. Reimbursement eligibility, receipt requirements, and claim limits are defined separately in the Reimbursement Policy.
