# Reimbursement Policy
**Policy ID:** FIN-REIM-001
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01
**Policy Owner:** Finance

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines reimbursement eligibility, documentation requirements, claim timelines, meal limits, hotel reimbursement, and non-reimbursable expenses.

## 2. General Reimbursement Rules
A reimbursement claim is eligible only if:
- the expense is business-related
- the expense has prior approval where required
- valid receipts or invoices are submitted
- the claim is submitted within the defined timeline
- the expense is within policy limits

Reimbursement approval is not guaranteed. Finance may reject claims that do not meet policy conditions.

## 3. Claim Submission Timeline
Employees must submit reimbursement claims within **15 calendar days** of expense date or travel completion date.

Late claims require finance approval and may be rejected if there is no valid reason for delay.

## 4. Receipt Requirements
Receipts are mandatory for:
- hotel stays
- local transport
- meals above INR 500
- client entertainment
- any claim above INR 1,000

Receipts must clearly show date, amount, vendor name, and expense description.

## 5. Meal Reimbursement
Meal reimbursement is allowed for approved business travel.

For **same-day domestic business travel**, employees may claim meals up to **INR 800 per day**, provided valid receipts are submitted.

For **overnight domestic travel**, employees may claim meals up to **INR 1,500 per day**, provided valid receipts are submitted.

Alcohol, tobacco, personal snacks, and non-business entertainment are not reimbursable.

## 6. Hotel Reimbursement
Hotel reimbursement is allowed only for approved overnight business travel.

Required documents for hotel reimbursement:
- hotel invoice
- payment receipt
- approved travel request
- check-in and check-out dates
- GST details where applicable

Hotel reimbursement is not available for same-day travel unless overnight stay was separately approved.

## 7. Local Transport Reimbursement
Local transport during business travel is reimbursable if supported by receipts or ride invoices.

Personal commuting between home and regular office location is not reimbursable.

## 8. Non-Reimbursable Expenses
The following expenses are not reimbursable:
- alcohol and tobacco
- personal entertainment
- personal shopping
- expenses without business purpose
- expenses without required approval
- fines, penalties, or traffic violations
- personal preference upgrades

## 9. Policy Exceptions
Any exception requires written approval from Finance.

Finance review is mandatory for claims that are unclear, incomplete, above policy limit, or outside standard policy rules.
